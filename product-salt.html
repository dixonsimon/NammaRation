About Iodized Iodized Salt

Iodized salt is table salt (sodium chloride) that has been fortified with a small, crucial amount of the essential trace mineral, iodine. It was developed as a simple and effective public health measure to combat widespread iodine deficiency, which can lead to serious health issues, including thyroid gland problems like goiter and developmental impairments. The iodine, typically added in the form of potassium iodate, is present in such minute quantities that it doesn't affect the salt's taste, texture, or culinary properties. It is used exactly like regular salt as an all-purpose seasoning in cooking and baking, all while providing a vital nutrient.

Key Features:

  • Standard table salt enhanced with the essential nutrient iodine.
  • Its main purpose is to prevent health issues like goiter and support proper thyroid function.
  • Often includes an agent to prevent clumping, ensuring it remains free-flowing.
  • Typically available as fine, uniform crystals that dissolve quickly.

Storage Instructions:

Store iodized salt in a tightly sealed container in a cool, dry place to prevent clumping and preserve the iodine.
