About Toor Dal

Toor dal, also known as arhar dal or split pigeon peas, is a staple yellow lentil in Indian cuisine, essential for making traditional dishes like sambar, dal tadka, and Gujarati dal.

Key Features:

  • It is the most popular and widely consumed pulse in India, forming a part of daily meals across the country.
  • Appears as small, yellow, oval-shaped split lentils.
  • Possesses a subtle taste that makes it a perfect canvas for spices and other seasonings.
  • A highly nutritious lentil, packed with plant-based protein, dietary fiber, and folic acid.

Storage Instructions:

Store dry toor dal in an airtight container in a cool, dark place, and refrigerate any cooked leftovers promptly.
