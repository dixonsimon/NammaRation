Account & Registration

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Profile" link in the navigation menu, then select "Create Account". You'll need to provide your basic information, ration card details, and a valid phone number for verification.

What documents do I need to register?

You'll need your ration card number, Aadhaar card details, and a valid phone number. In some cases, we might require additional verification documents which our team will inform you about during the registration process.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered phone number or email address, and we'll send you a verification code to reset your password.

Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?

Browse our products on the home page, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your delivery address and select a payment method. Once confirmed, your order will be processed and delivered to your doorstep.

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver across Bangalore city. Use our location page to check if we deliver to your area. We're continuously expanding our service areas, so check back regularly if we're not in your neighborhood yet.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 24-48 hours. For urgent requirements, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM, subject to availability in your area.

Products & Quality

How do you ensure product quality?

We source all our products directly from government-approved suppliers and FCI warehouses. Each product undergoes quality checks before packaging and delivery. We maintain strict quality control standards to ensure you receive fresh, authentic ration products.

What if I receive damaged or expired products?

We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you receive damaged, expired, or incorrect products, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund as per our return policy.

Can I check the product details before ordering?

Yes, each product page includes detailed information about the product, including brand, weight, expiry date (where applicable), and nutritional information. You can also view customer reviews for most products.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including cash on delivery, UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. All online payments are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed according to our refund policy. For online payments, refunds are credited back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. For cash payments, we process refunds via bank transfer or store credit.

I was charged but my order didn't go through. What should I do?

In rare cases, payment may be deducted but the order fails to process. Don't worry - the amount will be automatically refunded within 24 hours. If not, please contact our support team with your transaction details for immediate assistance.
