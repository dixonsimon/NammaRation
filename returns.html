Refunds & Returns Policy

We strive for complete customer satisfaction. Learn about our return and refund procedures.

Return Policy Overview

At Namma Ration, we take pride in the quality of our products and services. However, if you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help with our straightforward return and refund policy.

Quick Summary: Most products can be returned within 24 hours of delivery if they are damaged, incorrect, or defective. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Eligibility for Returns

You may return products under the following circumstances:

  • Damaged Products: Items that arrived broken, torn, or otherwise damaged
  • Incorrect Items: Products that differ from what you ordered
  • Expired Products: Items past their expiry date at time of delivery
  • Quality Issues: Products that don't meet our quality standards
  • Missing Items: If part of your order is missing

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of:

  • Opened food packages (unless defective)
  • Perishable items that have been delivered correctly
  • Products damaged due to customer mishandling
  • Items purchased more than 24 hours ago

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return a product:

1
Report Issue

Contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery

2
Provide Details

Share order information and photos of the issue

3
Approval

We'll review your request and approve if eligible

4
Resolution

We'll arrange pickup and process refund/replacement

Note: In most cases, we'll arrange for product pickup from your delivery address at no additional cost.

Refund Methods & Timelines

Refunds are processed based on your original payment method:

Payment Method Refund Method Processing Time
Credit/Debit Card Original Card 5-7 business days
UPI Payment Bank Account 3-5 business days
Net Banking Bank Account 3-5 business days
Digital Wallet Wallet Balance 24-48 hours
Cash on Delivery Bank Transfer 3-5 business days

Store Credit Option: For faster resolution, you may opt for store credit which is processed within 24 hours and can be used for future purchases.

Special Cases

Cancelled Orders

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled but can be returned after delivery following our standard return policy.

Failed Deliveries

If our delivery team is unable to deliver your order after multiple attempts, the order will be cancelled and a full refund will be processed.

Quality Complaints

For quality-related complaints, we may require you to provide photographs or retain the product for inspection by our quality team.
